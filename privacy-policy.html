1. Information We Collect

At WEST SHORELINE LABS, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how OneVerse collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account information (username, email address)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Communication preferences
  • Usage data and app interactions

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (operating system, app version)
  • Log data (access times, features used)
  • Analytics data to improve app performance
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the OneVerse app functionality
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Ensure app security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

OneVerse may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand app usage and improve performance. These services may collect anonymous usage data.

Cloud Services

Data may be stored on secure cloud platforms to ensure reliability and accessibility across devices.

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Secure data centers with physical and network security

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

OneVerse does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

Parents and guardians who believe their child has provided personal information to OneVerse should contact us immediately at the email address provided below.

If you are under 18, please obtain parental consent before using OneVerse or providing any personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any material changes through:

  • In-app notifications

  • Updates to this page with a revised "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of OneVerse after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
